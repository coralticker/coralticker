## Catching one before it's gone.

These move. The realistic play is a wishlist alert plus a rough sense of what it usually goes for, so you can act in the moment instead of refreshing five tabs. For the names that swing on price, the [price history](/coral/jf-homewrecker/price-history) tells you whether today's number is a genuine deal or just an ordinary Tuesday.

And if you're newer to acros, don't start here. Start with a piece you can afford to lose while you dial in flow and light, then come back for the chase corals once the tank's stable. A Walt Disney is a heartbreak coral in a system that isn't ready for it.
